## Method

0. Press <kbd>F12</kbd> to reach *developer toolbox*

1. Choose *Network*
2. :white_check_mark: Disable cache
3. Press and hold *reload button* on the left top of Chrome
4. Choose *Empty Cache and Hard Reload*
